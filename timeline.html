Total Number of Floods For Each Month From 1985 - 2020

The line graph beside show us the trend of the floods occurance for each month from 1985 until 2020. As we can see, there is a fluctuation happens from January until April where the occurance of floods on these months show a decreasing number then it increased again on the other month. The trend shows an increasing number starting from May until it reaches its peak on July. The occurance of floods will start to decrease after it reached its peak. The trend shows a downward trend from August until December which means that the floods will occur less frequent than the last month.
