There were few floods that were caused by:

  • High Tides
  • Avalanche
  • Tidal Surge
  • Landslide
  • Glacial Lake Outburst
  • Tsunami
  • Typhoon
  • Levee Failure
  • Storm Surge
  • Hurricane
  • Winter Storm


Each cause that has been listed above had not cause more than 10 cases of floods counted from 1985 - 2020

Some events cause more cases compared to the previous ones. Cyclone caused around 22 cases so far while Ice Jams are recorded to cause more cases, which is 32 cases of floods from 1985-2020. Rain and Snowmelts caused more number of floods with 48 cases were recorded to be caused by this event.

Several events are even causing more cases such as 79 cases were caused by Snowmelt, 154 cases were caused by Tropical Storm and 57 cases were recorded as floods that were caused by Dam/Levy Break or Release.

More and more floods are caused by the other issues and most of them are caused by rain such as Torrential Rain that has caused 539 cases so far, Moonson Rain with its 436 cases, Tropical Cyclone with 340 cases of floods while Brief Torrential Rain is recorded being able to cause 210 cases of floods from 1985 until 2020.

The Main Causes of floods that have been recorded is

Heavy Rain

Unexpectedly, there were around 2998 cases that have been recorded that were caused by Heavy Rain. We cannot do a lot of thing to control the intensity of a rain, but since we have known that heavy rain is the main cause of most floods in that have been happened, we need to pay extra attention on our waterways to make sure that the water will not overflow when a heavy is coming and we also need to prepare our environment to store the water volume that will be brought by the heavy rain by doing Reforestation.

Let us save people and save the world from floods. Let us be the changes.
